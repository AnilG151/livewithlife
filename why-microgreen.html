Why Microgreens?

Microgreens are nutrient-dense young plants. They are packed with vitamins (A, B, C, K, etc.), minerals (Calcium, Iron, Magnesium), and antioxidants. Including microgreens in your daily diet supports immunity, digestion, heart health, and overall well-being.
