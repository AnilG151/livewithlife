Why Microgreens?

Microgreens contain 4–40× more vitamins, minerals and antioxidants compared to mature greens — harvest in 7–14 days and use fresh.

  • ✔ Boost Immunity
  • ✔ Support Heart Health
  • ✔ Aid Digestion
  • ✔ Rich in Natural Antioxidants

How to use

Sprinkle on salads, blend into smoothies, top soups & sandwiches.
