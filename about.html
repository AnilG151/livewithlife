About Us

Life With Leaf is committed to bringing you fresh, pesticide-free microgreens. We also provide seeds and grow kits so you can cultivate your own microgreens at home. Our mission is to make healthy living easy, natural, and accessible for everyone.

Our Vision

To inspire a healthier lifestyle by promoting the growth and consumption of nutrient-dense microgreens.

Our Values

  • Freshness and quality
  • Sustainability and eco-friendly practices
  • Transparency and customer trust
