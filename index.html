Varieties like red cabbage microgreens help lower LDL (bad) cholesterol and reduce inflammation, supporting a healthy heart.

Help Manage Type 2 Diabetes

Microgreens like broccoli and fenugreek may improve insulin sensitivity and help regulate blood sugar levels.

Boost Brain Function

Aid Digestion & Gut Health

High in dietary fiber, microgreens support healthy digestion and act as prebiotics for beneficial gut bacteria.
